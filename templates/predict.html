About Stroke

A stroke occurs when blood flow to the brain is blocked or a blood vessel bursts. Early risk detection can be life-saving.

Every 40 sec a stroke occurs
80% are preventable
5th leading cause of death
Key Risk Factors
Advanced age (65+)
Hypertension
Heart disease
High glucose (diabetes)
Obesity (BMI > 30)
Smoking
Prevention Tips
Control blood pressure
Exercise 30 min/day
Manage blood sugar
Eat a balanced diet
Quit smoking
Limit alcohol intake
